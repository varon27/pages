In a nutshell

After 10 years in marketing and communications, and as much time spent working with digital teams (developers, engineers, game designers, gamers...), I wanted more than beeing a witness.

End of 2017, I decide to take an active share by learning how to develop.
First, the basics, Html, Css, PhP, some JS... just what is needed to build a website from scratch... and make it work! The result is awesome, it feels like I'm doing magic and I want to know more.

Sure thing, I'm definitely more into front-end, passionate about building/bringing to life beautiful UI and interactions, that's why I focused on Javascript through the VueJs framework.

And guess what? I love it more and more. Building smart reusable components, thinking the architecture of an app with regards to performance, design patterns, security and great code quality makes me feel accomplished and always eager to go deeper and better.

In the mean time, I always wanted to live a life abroad, and what's best than web development to allow you to work anywhere in the world using a universal language?!

That's why I moved to Perth, Western Australia.

My new home town drives me toward one an only one goal: Strengthen my Javascript knowledge in contact with Australian developers and settle down in the country for good.
